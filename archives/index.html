How to prepare & deliver a talk

  • Nothing to do with your English. (?)

Structure: how to organize your talk

  • A single thesis statement
    • Everything supports your thesis
  • Make your talk prefix-competitive
  • Put your results up front
  • Say what you did, why you did it, but not how you did it

  • Give your results multiple times at different levels/granularities

  • Begin a talk: explain your title

  • Show scholarship
    • how does your work fit into the rest of the world? (Solved an important open problem)
  • Go deep rather than board
  • How to prepare: explain your talk to others
  • View your talk as a tree traversal

How your slides should look like

  • slide deck/slides should be self contained
  • slide should have a point and a conclusion
  • nearly every slide should have a picture/example
  • the last slide should convey what you want people to see at the end

Hint to Deliver Your Talk

  • Keeping people’s attention
  • People only pay attention to one thing at a time
  • Looking at people’s faces. Stand by the screen, point and touch the screen.
  • Conclude your slide

How to present graphs

  • Explain axes (mention units)
  • Mention which way is good

  • Explain meanings

  • Tell personal stories (be careful)

  • Smile
  • Should you read your slides?
    • Should be a super set
  • Tell a joke
    • commit to your joke yourself
    • explain it the way that audience know when to laugh
  • Don’t apologize

十年

距离那场考试已经整整十年了。依稀还记得些奇怪的细节：考试后的细雨；特地找到某个考室却还是没确定里面坐的那位是否是多年不见的一位朋友，反而差点迟到了数学考试；语文 的作文题“初中●高中“；午饭的鱼头；去往考场的长长的阶梯；当然还有年少轻狂的自己。

那时候一起去考试的小伙伴们，都已渐渐没了联系。留在记忆里的，只剩下一串串名字和一张张稚气面孔。初中的往事大多已模糊，却还有些无法忘记的画面惹人感慨。正如现在每次 踏上学校新建的人工草场时，都仿佛闻到了十年前第一次比赛时福州大学里草场一模一样的味道。

还记得初中入学测试我帮忙出语文卷子却考得让人哭笑不得；

还记得晚自习之后在家一边看天下足球一边写周记；

Setting up Hadoop (fedora-linux)

Choose your way of installation

There are actually two ways of installing Hadoop:

  • Download source code and compile it;
  • Download distribution and use it.

The problem of the 2nd approach is that the pre-compiled distribution is a 32-bit version, and it generates a warning every time a command is executed when running on a 64-bit machine. But generally you would get into much more troubles if you try to compile from the source code. So here we use the 2nd approach and just ignore the warnings. The distribution can be downloaded from hadoop's website. In case you want to access API docs in an IDE such as eclipse, you can also download the source code and attach it to appropriate packages.
